SenseBox

The senseBox group takes its name from the SenseBox project developed by the Institute for Geoinformatics in the University of Münster. “The senseBox is a do-it-yourself kit for stationary and mobile sensor stations”. Our group used the senseBox to build different kind of sensors to monitor the environmental quality of the river Aa study area. The sensors built were: a stationary weather logger, a stationary water level logger, a mobile air quality logger mounted on a drone, and a mobile pH and water quality logger.
