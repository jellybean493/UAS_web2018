Ellipse Portal

Welcome to our Ellipse portal project.
This web portal intends to provide visualization and processing tools for public users, from our processed information. The main objective of develop project was the application and develop exploratory approaches using several geoespacial technologies, to monitor renaturation process in the Aa River, located in city of Munster, Germany. We took part of the Unmanned Aerial Systems for Applied Research Summer Semester Course 2018, held in the University of Munster - Institute for Geoinformatics under the guidance of Professor Jan Lehmann, Professor Torsten Prinz and Professor Sergey Mukhametov. The information displayed is a result of a extensive joint work, since our class was divided in several groups: Esri Cloud, Sense&Box, Image Analysis, Sensors and Platforms, Lidar, Project Management, Web Portal.
